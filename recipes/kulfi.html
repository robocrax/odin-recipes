Kulfi

Description

Kesar Badam Kulfi is a delicious frozen Indian dessert with the creamy texture of whole-milk, the crunch of almonds combined with a hint of saffron & cardamom.

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 litres Whole Milk
  • 1/2-3/4 cups Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Almonds (Badam), Blanched and Chopped
  • A few strands of Saffron - Dissolved in Milk
  • 3 Bread Slices

Steps

  1. Boil milk on medium flame till it has reduced to half its original quantity.
  2. Add sugar and boil for another 10 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and allow it to cool.
  4. Add prepared almonds and saffron.
  5. Remove crust from bread slices and soak in quarter cup of milk for 15 minutes.
  6. Make a fine pulp of this in a blender and add this to the cooled milk.
  7. Pour in kulfi molds or in a tiffin box and leave it in a freezer to set for about 8 hours.
